Merlin R Farnham

- Digital Marketer - Brand Builder - Storyteller -

Hi, I’m Merlin! I’m a senior marketing student at the University of Akron, specializing in market research and digital strategy. I turn research and raw data into comprehensive, holistic marketing campaigns that elevate brand recognition and build genuine customer connections.

My marketing journey began when I was appointed Publications Chairman for my fraternity chapter. Leading a dedicated committee, I learned to navigate the fast-paced world of social media management, event promotion, and public relations. Three years later, I have found my stride in the digital marketing space, and I look forward to leveraging my unique experience and toolkit to drive measurable growth for forward-thinking brands.

The Alpha Omichronicle Newsletter

Because of B.R.A.Y.D.E.N. IMC Campaign
